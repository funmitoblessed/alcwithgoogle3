Dolphins

A group of dolphins is called a "school" or "pod". Male dolphins are called "bulls", females are called "cows" and young dolphins are called "calves".

Scientific Name: Delphinus delphis
Average Weight: 80 - 150 kg
Average Lifespan: 40 - 45 years
Habitat: Temperate and Tropical Waters

Dolphins are often regarded as one of Earth's most intelligent animals. They are highly social and establish very strong bonds. They are also known to stay with ill or injured individuals, even helping them to breathe by bringing them to the surface if needed.
